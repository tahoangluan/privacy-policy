Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Overview

Lumière Expenses is a mobile application designed to help users track expenses by scanning receipts. Your privacy is important to us. This app does not collect, store, or share personal data.

2. Camera Usage

The app uses your device’s camera solely to capture images of receipts for expense tracking. Images are processed locally on your device. No images are uploaded to any server.

3. Photo Library Access

If you choose to import images from your photo library, the app will access only the images you select. No other data is accessed or stored.

4. Data Processing

All receipt scanning and text recognition (OCR) is performed locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

5. Data Storage

All expense data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data.

6. Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
